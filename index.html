Objective: There are a vast variety of marijuana strains. Like wine, strains have their own unique smell, taste, and effects on consumption. We want this data visualization to help the user discover new strains that may align with their preferrences. Users may filter the data visualization by type, rating, effects, and flavor.


My data source:

  • https://www.kaggle.com/kingburrito666/cannabis-strains/data
  • https://www.leafly.com/explore/sort-alpha
  • https://herb.co/cannabis/strains

  • Type

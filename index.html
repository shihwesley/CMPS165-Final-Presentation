Cannabis Strains: Emotions and Medical Relief

Cannabis, also known as Marijuana, Pot, or Weed, is a psychoactive drug from the Cannabis plant that changes the
brain's functions, resulting in alterations in perception, mood, and more. The main reason for the alterations is
Tetrahydrocannabinol, or THC. THC can alter the brain's functions so that users can experience different feelings.
Some enjoy different effects, so they take it for recreational purposes. Others require the drug, for medical purposes.
It can be used to treat a number of problems, such as muscle spasms, or even chronic pain. Being legalized in the recent
years, Cannabis is now available for anyone over 21 years old. There are over 2400 strains, providing 2400 different
effects. Go try some, and see what's best for you!
